Colgate is an american brand principally used for oral hygiene products such as toothpaste, toothbrushes, mouthwashes and
dental floss. Manufactured by colgate palmolive, colgate and oral hygiene products were first sold by the company in 1873,
sixteen years after the death of the founder, William Colgate.
